WHERE SINGAPORE COMES TO PLAY


Places of interests

National Stadium


As the centrepiece of the Singapore Sports Hub, the 55,000 capacity National Stadium has played host to the best in Live Sports and Entertainment events such as the 28th Southeast Asian Games, International Champions Cup, HSBC Singapore Rugby Sevens, BTS, Exo and Jay Chou.
The National Stadium also has a retractable roof and movable tiered seating, making it the only stadium in the world that is custom-designed to host different sporting events at any given time.








Singapore Indoor Stadium


The iconic Indoor Stadium has hosted a wide variety of sports and entertainment events, including Western and Asian superstars, family entertainment, award shows, sports matches and other large-scale events.
It has a capacity of 12,000 and was ranked as the 150th busiest arena venue in the world for 2015, with 72,342 tickets being sold for the entire year.









OCBC Arena


Comprising two blocks of multi-purpose halls, the OCBC Arena accomodates a full range of sporting activities. Whether you're an athlete or a sports enthusiast, amp up your fitness level at the OCBC Arena today.
The OCBC Arena is the proud home of six National Sports Association including the Singapore Slingers. The facilities at the Singapore Sports Hub are built for multi-purpose usage that includes community/public usage and elite athletes training.








OCBC Aquatic Centre


Host to many marquee Aquatic events, the OCBC Aquatic Centre also offers a wide range of professional aquatic facilities for you to enjoy. Now you can choose to train while your family enjoys a relaxing swim!
The OCBC Aquatic Centre is located right beside Singapore Indoor Stadium and has a capacity of 3000. This FINA international standards pool is home to Singapore Swimming Association (SSA), serving as the hosting venue for the 28th South-East Asian Games 2015 and 5th FINA Juniors Swimming Championships 2015.








Splash-N-Surf


Escape into a world of water-soaked fun at the Splash-N-Surf! From the adrenaline-pumping Stingray to the ever popular Lazy River, explore a variety of fun recreational water activities with family and friends!
Bring on your sense of adventure and enjoy a variety of recreational water activities at the Singapore Sports Hub! Whether you're young or young-at-heart, have a splashing good time with your family and friends at our thrilling outdoor water play areas!








Water Sports Centre


Rent a boat of your choice at the Water Sports Centre and enjoy a perfect day out on the water. Bring along your sense of adventure, and paddle down all 35 hectares of the beautiful Kallang Basin!
Perfect for the community and athletes, the Water Sports Centre offers a variety of canoes and kayaks for rent. If you're up for a challenge, compete with your family and friends at the 500-metre Regatta course mapped out on the Kallang Basin!
For an immersive water sports experience, be sure to check out our variety of water sports courses!








Singapore Sports Museum


Ever wondered what it's like to be a sporting hero? Explore the history of sports at the Singapore Sports Museum and find out how sports has changed Singapore today!
Immerse in the world of sports and explore exciting and interactive exhibits!
Dedicated to telling the stories of Singapore's sporting aspirations, the Singapore Sports Museum is Singapore's first sports-related Museum and welcomes sports training academics, international sporting organisations, as well as curious minds from locally and abroad!








Sports Hub Library


Looking for a place to catch up on your weekend read? Enrich your mind or have fun with family and friends on our life sized chess set at the Sports Hub Library!










Kallang Wave Mall

The one stop destination to shop, dine and play, that provides a vibrant and engaging lifestyle experiences all round. Whether you are seeking out the best flavours, or love venturing into heart-pumping adventures, there's always something to do for everyone at Kallang Wave Mall, Singapore Indoor Stadium and Water Sports Centre.
Shops include Singapore's first sports-themed hypermart, FairPrice Xtra, fashion icons H&M and Cotton On, My Kampung, a halal foodcourt run by Yu Kee Group, Japanese casual wear designer and retailer UNIQLO, as well as Singapore's first authentic Japanese onsen and spa - Yunomori Onsen & Spa.
